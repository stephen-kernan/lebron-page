LEBRON JAMES

The Greatest Basketball Player of Our Generation

Lebron James is currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers- one of the NBA's most storied and accomplished franchises.

    Here is a timeline of Lebron's life and career:

  • 1984: Born in Akron, Ohio
  • 2003: Selected by the Cleveland Cavaliers with the first overall pick in the NBA Draft
  • 2003: Won the Rookie of the Year Award
  • 2009: Won his first MVP award
  • 2010: Won his second MVP award
  • 2010: Lebron announces his intent to sign with the Miami Heat
  • 2012: Won his first NBA Championship with the Miami Heat
  • 2012: Won his third MVP award.
  • 2012: Won his first NBA Finals MVP award
  • 2013: Won his second NBA championship with the Miami Heat
  • 2013: Won his fourth MVP award.
  • 2012: Won his second NBA Finals MVP award
  • 2014: Signed with the Cleveland Cavaliers
  • 2016: Won his third championship. This gave Cleveland their first major sports championship in 52 years by beating the Golden State Warriors in one of the most impressive upsets in NBA History
  • 2018: Signed with the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2018: Opened and fully funded the I Promise School in Akron, Ohio- a public school in which each student receives a free bike, free meals, and college tuition.
